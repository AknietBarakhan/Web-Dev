Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 2 pieces (around 400g)
Pasta (penne or fettuccine) 300g
Heavy cream 200ml
Parmesan cheese 50g, grated
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Salt & pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Heat olive oil in a pan, cook the chicken until golden and fully cooked, then remove and slice.

Step 3: In the same pan, saut√© garlic until fragrant, add heavy cream and parmesan, stir until creamy.

Step 4: Add pasta and chicken back into the pan, toss to combine and coat with sauce. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 5: Serve hot with extra parmesan or fresh herbs if desired.
